VectorLabel is installed

The Engine runs in your menu bar (top‑right) and drives the printer — it should have started automatically. If you don't see its icon, open /Applications/VectorLabel/VectorLabelEngine.app.

  • Open the designers from the Engine menu, or directly from /Applications/VectorLabel/.
  • If the menu‑bar icon is hidden by a crowded menu bar, re‑opening the Engine opens its Preferences window, or enable “Show in Dock” there.
  • Vectorworks plug‑ins (if installed): restart Vectorworks, then add the two “Export … Circuits to VectorLabel” commands to your menu via Tools ▸ Workspaces ▸ Edit Current Workspace.

Thank you for using VectorLabel.
