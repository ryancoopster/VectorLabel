VectorLabel

This installs the VectorLabel suite for designing and printing labels on Brady M610 / M611 and Brother P‑touch printers.

Four apps are installed into /Applications/VectorLabel/:

  • Engine — the menu‑bar app that drives the printer and the print queue (always on).
  • Auto Print — watches exported data and prints in batches.
  • Template Designer — design reusable label templates.
  • Custom Designer — design one‑off labels from your own data.

On the next screen you'll review the license. You can optionally also install a set of starter label templates.

VectorLabel is source‑available software. Free to use, including commercially; not for resale. Provided without warranty.
